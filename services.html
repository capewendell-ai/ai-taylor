From basic call handling to fully integrated enterprise solutions, our AI attendants ensure you never miss a customer opportunity. Each package is designed to deliver immediate ROI while scaling with your business needs.

Bronze – AI Attendant Lite

$120/month • Setup Fee: $150

A simple AI phone/website attendant that answers calls, takes orders or messages, and sends them via email or text. No integrations — purely capture & relay.

Features:

  • Answers calls 24/7
  • Takes orders, questions, or messages
  • Sends email/SMS notifications to staff
  • Basic personality customization (voice, tone)
  • Call logs & transcripts

ROI Statement:

"If your business misses even 5 calls a week worth $50 each, that's $1,000/month in lost revenue — this system ensures you never miss a lead again, paying for itself in less than a month."

Silver – AI Attendant Pro

$399/month • Setup Fee: $300

Attendant that not only answers calls but manages scheduling in Google Calendar, Outlook, or booking systems and sends confirmations automatically.

Features:

  • All Bronze features
  • Integrates with your scheduling system or Google Calendar
  • Sends booking confirmations & reminders (email/SMS)
  • Custom FAQs (product details, store hours, policies)
  • Call routing to humans when necessary
  • Basic analytics dashboard (call volume, peak times)

ROI Statement:

"Reducing no-shows by 20% can add hundreds or thousands per month in recovered revenue. If a missed appointment costs $75, and you save 10 no-shows a month, you've just covered the cost 1.5x over."
